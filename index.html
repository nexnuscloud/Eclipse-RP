Frequently Asked Questions

Check out some of the most frequently asked questions below about our FiveM server.

  • What is the pricing for joining the server?

    Joining our FiveM server is absolutely free! For additional perks or in-game purchases, feel free to check out our store or join our Discord Server for more details.

  • Do I need Discord to play or get involved in the FiveM server?

    Yes, Discord is a requirement to fully participate in the FiveM server community, stay updated with announcements, and connect with other players. Join our Discord Server to get started.

    You can reach out to us through our contact form or join our Discord Server to create a support ticket.

    If you wish to join our staff team, you must apply through the Discord Server and follow the instructions in the staff recruitment channel.
